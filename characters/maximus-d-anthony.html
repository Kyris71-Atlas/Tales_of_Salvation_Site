Character Discovery

Identity

  • Human Variant, later marked Fiend
  • Fighter 11 / Paladin 2
  • Noble background
  • Deity: Helm

Character Sheet Notes

  • Tall, toned, dark-skinned fighter
  • Youngest son of a lesser noble family
  • Fought to defend himself from older brothers
  • Wanted to make a name for himself

Personality

  • Did not place himself above common folk
  • Wanted independence from his family
  • Loved the heir of a rival family
  • Sharp tongue often got him in trouble

Campaign Transformation

  • Sought to be a champion for the little guy
  • Measured himself against truly good companions
  • Leaned into the bad-boy persona
  • Learned he liked the power and fear
Player Perspective
Max wanted to be a champion and stand up for ordinary people. When he came face to face with Lulu and Cedric — characters who embodied genuine goodness — he realized he was not that kind of hero. As he leaned into the darker persona, he discovered he liked it.

Notable Equipment

Curated items selected by Atlas using Fantasy Grounds data, Atlas Portal guidance, and campaign-specific meaning.

  • Shield of the Hidden Lord — Max had a working relationship with the shield until releasing it to Zariel's care.
  • Devil's Ride / Pit Ride — Max loved riding his Devil's Ride across Avernus. The campaign archive preserves a customized Devil's Ride record named Pit Ride.
  • Ride Control Amulet — Campaign adaptation inspired by the Shield Guardian control amulet concept.
  • Signet Ring — Important personal symbol from Max's minor noble family.
  • Hellrazor — Notable weapon carried during the archived campaign.

Campaign Highlights

  • Discovered the Shield of the Hidden Lord in the Vanthampur Vault.
  • Rode his Devil's Ride across Avernus as part of his identity.
  • Helped reunite Zariel with her sword and save Elturel.
  • Developed an Aura of Fear and came to enjoy infernal power.
  • At the conclusion of Mortal Ascension, made a bargain with Asmodeus and became a Lord of Hell.

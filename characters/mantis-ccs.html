5 · Metamagic Font of Magic

Mantis has two different fuels. Don’t mix them up:

1 · Spell slots
Track above under Combat

Spend a slot to cast a spell of that level (or lower). Example: Lightning Bolt needs a 3rd-level slot. Long rest → all slots back.

2 · Sorcery points
This section · max 5 at level 5

Spend points for metamagic (cooler casts) or Flexible Casting (trade points ↔ slots). Long rest → all points back.

Sorcery points
Points left: / 5

Start each day at 5. Tap the number down when you spend. Long rest → set back to 5. (No Ready/Used button — the number is the track.)

Flexible Casting · Create a slot
Bonus action · spend points → gain a spell slot

Out of slots but need to cast? Buy a slot with points:

  • 1st-level slotcosts 2 points
  • 2nd-level slotcosts 3 points
  • 3rd-level slotcosts 5 points

Then cast your spell and spend that new slot as usual. Created slots vanish if unused when you finish a long rest.

Flexible Casting · Slot → points
Bonus action · burn a spell slot → gain points

Need points for metamagic? Convert a slot you aren’t using:

  • 1st-level slotgives 2 points
  • 2nd-level slotgives 3 points
  • 3rd-level slotgives 5 points

Lower the slot track above, raise sorcery points here (max 5). You can’t go over 5 points.

Quick recipes (level 5)
· Need one more 1st-level cast? −2 points → +1 1st slot → cast
· Need points for Twinned 1st? burn a 1st slot (+2 pts) or 2nd (+3)
· Want Empowered Lightning Bolt? Cast with a 3rd slot, then spend 1 point to reroll damage dice

Metamagic · Empowered Spell
When you roll damage · 1 point

Reroll up to 4 damage dice (CHA mod); must use the new rolls. Great with Lightning Bolt / Chaos Bolt.

Metamagic · Twinned Spell
When you cast · points = spell level (cantrip = 1)

Spell must target only one creature. Twin it onto a second creature in range. Example: twin a 1st-level single-target spell → spend 1 point + the slot.

Tempestuous Magic
Bonus action · after casting a 1st+ spell · free (not points)

Fly up to 10 ft without opportunity attacks (table note: BG3-style 30 ft if using that house rule). This is storm sorcerer, not Font of Magic — no sorcery points.

Lucky (Halfling)
When you roll a 1 on a d20

Reroll the die; must use the new roll.

Brave · Naturally Stealthy
Halfling · Lightfoot

Brave: advantage on saves vs frightened. Naturally Stealthy: can attempt to hide when obscured only by a creature at least one size larger.

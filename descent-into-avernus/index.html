Mortal Ascension

The campaign that followed Zariel's redemption and the salvation of Elturel.

Mortal Ascension continued the story after the heroes reunited Zariel with her sword, witnessed her redemption, and saved Elturel. With the fate of a city secured, the story turned toward the heroes themselves and the choices that would shape their futures.

The Spice Boys

Without discussing character concepts beforehand, all five players independently created human male characters.

The coincidence quickly became a running joke. A comparison to the Spice Girls gave birth to the party name that would follow them through two campaigns:

The Spice Boys

What began as a joke became the company that saved Elturel and continued their adventures into Mortal Ascension.

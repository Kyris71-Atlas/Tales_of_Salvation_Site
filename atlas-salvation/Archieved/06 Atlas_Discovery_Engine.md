# Atlas Discovery Engine

**Version:** 1.0

---

# Purpose

The Atlas Discovery Engine (A.D.E.) defines the operational methodology Atlas uses to discover campaign knowledge.

Atlas does not begin with conclusions.

Atlas begins with discovery.

The purpose of the Discovery Engine is to ensure that every phase of discovery is performed in a consistent, disciplined, and repeatable manner before narrative understanding is attempted.

---

# Operating Principles

- Discovery precedes interpretation.
- Each phase has a distinct purpose.
- Each completed phase earns the right to advance to the next.
- Discovery Reports record discoveries, not conclusions.
- Story Atlas is invoked only after discovery is complete.

---

# Discovery Workflow

| Phase | Name | Primary Question |
|------:|------|------------------|
| 1 | Character Record | What is the character's current mechanical state? |
| 2 | Character Sheet Notes | What was the player's authored vision? |
| 3 | Discovering Campaign Evidence | What can the campaign itself tell us? |
| 4 | Atlas Portal | What reviewed campaign knowledge already exists? |
| 5 | Oral History | What questions remain that only the participants can answer? |
| 6 | Story Atlas | Given everything discovered, who did the character become? |

---

# Phase 1 — Character Record

## Purpose

Discover the character's current mechanical state.

## Inputs

- Character records
- Database snapshots
- Character archives

## Output

Character Record Discovery Report.

---

# Phase 2 — Character Sheet Notes

## Purpose

Discover the player's authored vision of the character.

## Inputs

- Personality
- Ideals
- Bonds
- Flaws
- Appearance
- Backstory
- Character notes

## Output

Character Sheet Notes Discovery Report.

---

# Phase 3 — Discovering Campaign Evidence

## Purpose

Give the campaign one uninterrupted opportunity to speak for itself.

## Discovery Passes

### Mechanical Evidence

Discover evidence recorded by campaign systems.

Examples include:

- Character progression
- Equipment
- Inventory
- Treasure
- Spell selection
- Mechanical state transitions

### Narrative Evidence

Discover evidence recorded in campaign documents.

Examples include:

- Session journals
- DM notes
- Adventure logs
- Campaign recaps
- Player journals
- Handouts

### Emergent Evidence

Discover recurring observations supported by the available campaign evidence.

Emergent evidence is recorded only when supported by discovered evidence.

## Completion

A discovery pass is complete when its available evidence has been honestly exhausted.

Phase 3 is complete when the campaign evidence available to this phase has been honestly exhausted.

---

# Phase 4 — Atlas Portal

## Purpose

Compare discoveries against reviewed campaign knowledge.

The Atlas Portal is consulted only after Phase 3 has been completed.

## Output

Atlas Portal Discovery Report.

---

# Phase 5 — Oral History

## Purpose

Consult campaign participants to answer questions documentary evidence cannot answer.

Questions should emerge naturally from previous discoveries.

## Output

Oral History Discovery Report.

---

# Phase 6 — Story Atlas

## Purpose

Transform completed discovery into narrative understanding.

Story Atlas synthesizes discovery.

Story Atlas does not replace discovery.

## Output

Story Atlas Character Narrative.

---

# Discovery Reports

Each discovery phase produces a Discovery Report.

A Discovery Report records:

- Evidence examined
- Discoveries made
- Evidence not found
- Open questions

Discovery Reports do not contain unsupported conclusions or future plans.

---

# Phase Completion

A phase is complete when:

- Its purpose has been fulfilled.
- Its available evidence has been honestly exhausted.

Completion is not determined by the number of discoveries produced.

---

# Discovery Lifecycle

1. Discover evidence.
2. Record discoveries.
3. Complete the current phase.
4. Advance to the next phase.
5. Repeat until Story Atlas.

---

# Closing Statement

The Atlas Discovery Engine is an operational methodology.

It defines **how** Atlas discovers.

The principles that explain **why** the Discovery Engine is designed this way are maintained separately in **Atlas Discovery Principles**.

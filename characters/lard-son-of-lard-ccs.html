Magic Initiate (Sorcerer) uses CHA (+1). The journal is its own magic item — not the feat.

T. TeaLeaf’s Journal
Magic item · Reaction · 1 / short rest · no attunement

Cast Protection from Evil and Good (concentration, up to 10 minutes). Aberrations, celestials, elementals, fey, fiends, and undead have disadvantage on attacks against the target; the target can’t be possessed, charmed, or frightened by them. Once used, the journal does not work again until you finish a short rest.

Cantrips at will · Magic Initiate

Action · 120 ft · CHA spell attack

Ranged spell attack; on a hit, 2d8 (level 5) damage of a type you choose: acid, cold, fire, lightning, poison, psychic, or thunder. On a d8 roll of 8, roll another d8 and add it (up to CHA mod extra dice).

Friends
Action · Concentration, up to 1 minute

Advantage on all Charisma checks directed at one creature that isn’t hostile toward you. When the spell ends, the creature realizes you used magic and becomes hostile.

Shield
Magic Initiate · Reaction · 1 / long rest · CHA

When hit by an attack or targeted by Magic Missile: +5 AC until the start of your next turn (includes the triggering attack), and you take no damage from Magic Missile. Feat casting only — once until you finish a long rest.

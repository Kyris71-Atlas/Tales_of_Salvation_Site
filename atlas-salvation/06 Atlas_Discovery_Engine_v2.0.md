# Atlas Discovery Engine

**Project:** Atlas Salvation\
**Version:** 2.0\
**Status:** Living Document

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Purpose

The Atlas Discovery Engine (A.D.E.) defines the operational methodology
Atlas uses to discover, interpret, and synthesize campaign knowledge.

Atlas does not begin with conclusions.

Atlas begins with discovery.

Every phase has a single purpose, a defined set of inputs, and a clear
completion criterion.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Operating Principles

-   Discovery precedes interpretation.
-   Interpretation precedes synthesis.
-   Every phase has a distinct responsibility.
-   Evidence and context are never conflated.
-   Human experience completes discovery.
-   Discovery Reports record discoveries, not conclusions.

# Discovery Window

Phases 1--3 form the **Discovery Window**.

Only evidence gathered during this window may contribute to Discovery
Reports.

When Phase 3 is complete, Atlas performs **Discovery Closure**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Phase 1 --- Character Record

Purpose: Discover the current mechanical state.

Inputs: - Character records - Database snapshots - Character archives

Output: - Character Record Discovery Report

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Phase 2 --- Character Sheet Notes

Purpose: Discover the player's authored vision.

Inputs include: - Personality - Ideals - Bonds - Flaws - Appearance -
Backstory - Character notes

Output: - Character Sheet Notes Discovery Report

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Phase 3 --- Campaign Discovery

Purpose: Allow the campaign to speak for itself.

## Pass 1 --- Mechanical Evidence

Character progression, inventory, equipment, treasure, spells,
conditions and other recorded mechanics.

## Pass 2 --- Narrative Evidence

Session journals, DM notes, adventure logs, campaign recaps, player
journals and handouts.

## Pass 3 --- Emergent Patterns

Identify recurring patterns supported by the discovered evidence.

Emergent patterns are discoveries only when supported by evidence. They
are never speculation.

Output: - Campaign Discovery Report

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Discovery Closure

Discovery is complete when available evidence has been honestly
exhausted.

After Discovery Closure:

-   Discovery evidence is frozen.
-   Later context cannot rewrite Discovery.
-   Atlas transitions from evidence collection to interpretation.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Phase 4 --- Atlas Portal

Purpose: Build understanding through context.

## Portal Acquisition

Fantasy Grounds Portal Retrieval Procedure
1. Parse the current Fantasy Grounds db.xml directly.
2. Locate the Atlas Portal index.
3. Extract each linked internal recordname.
4. Resolve every reference against the matching XML branch.
5. Read the complete contents of each Portal entry.
6. Recursively resolve links contained inside Portal entries when relevant.
7. Preserve the full entry before interpreting or filtering it.
8. Report unresolved references explicitly.
Semantic search may help locate Portal material, but direct XML traversal is the authoritative retrieval method.
Portal titles must not be treated as content categories. A Portal entry may contain any information Christopher chose to share with Atlas from inside Fantasy Grounds.

Atlas first acquires the complete Atlas Portal.

Workflow:

1.  Locate the Portal index.
2.  Resolve every linked Portal record.
3.  Assemble the complete Portal.
4.  Review the complete Portal.
5.  Focus on the target character within the complete context.

Atlas never searches only by character name or note title.

## Context Review

Portal material may include:

-   Atlas Portal notes
-   Collaboration discussions
-   Session recaps
-   DM observations
-   Design discussions
-   Player reflections

These materials provide context only.

They never become Discovery evidence.

## Reflection

Atlas identifies themes, unanswered questions, and areas for human
validation.

Output: - Atlas Portal Interpretation Report

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Phase 5 --- Oral History

Purpose:

Consult campaign participants.

Questions should emerge naturally from Discovery and Portal review.

Output: - Oral History Report

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Phase 6 --- Story Atlas

Purpose:

Synthesize Discovery, Interpretation, and Oral History into a coherent
character narrative.

Story Atlas explains the journey.

It never replaces the underlying discovery.

Output: - Character Dossier / Story Atlas Narrative

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Reports

Each phase produces an artifact appropriate to its purpose.

-   Discovery Reports (Phases 1--3)
-   Atlas Portal Interpretation Report (Phase 4)
-   Oral History Report (Phase 5)
-   Story Atlas Narrative (Phase 6)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Completion Criteria

A phase is complete when:

-   Its purpose has been fulfilled.
-   Its available information has been honestly exhausted.
-   Its outputs are sufficient for the next phase.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Closing Statement

The Atlas Discovery Engine defines **how Atlas works**.

It preserves a disciplined boundary between discovery, interpretation,
and storytelling, ensuring every Character Dossier is traceable back to
evidence while remaining enriched by human experience.

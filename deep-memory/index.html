Deep Memory

Every campaign is worth keeping.

What this vault is

Deep Memory is the site-wide archive. It holds what the table lived: session recaps, Character Discovery Reports (dossiers), companions, and the threads that connect them.

It is not a play sheet. For the active table’s Company Character Sheets, start on Forge of Fury Campaign Home.

Open a chamber door to explore one campaign’s memory. Company Character Sheets (CCS) exist only for Tempest Company while Forge of Fury is the active campaign.

Three Strands of Memory

Company Character Sheet

Table tool for Tempest Company PCs — fast stats for play. Active campaign only. Not used for archived campaigns or NPC companions.

Discovery Report

Atlas archive page — evidence, story, and meaning as they are earned. Lives under Deep Memory. Grows over time; incomplete is honest.

Companions

Always NPCs. Some use a Fantasy Grounds character sheet for convenience — they remain companions, not company player characters. No CCS.

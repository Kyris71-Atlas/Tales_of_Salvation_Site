# Atlas Ground Truth

**Project:** Atlas Salvation\
**Version:** 0.3\
**Status:** Living Document

## Core Architectural Truths

-   Shared understanding precedes automation.
-   Fantasy Grounds owns mechanics.
-   Atlas owns meaning.
-   Atlas observes before it interprets.
-   Atlas transforms source-system data into durable knowledge.
-   Atlas Notes are a curated communication channel between the Dungeon
    Master and Atlas.
-   Atlas Notes are intentionally authored to guide Atlas Discovery and
    are prioritized during the discovery process.
-   Player Notes provide valuable player perspective and story context
    but are not promoted to campaign canon without supporting evidence.
-   The user manages one file. Atlas manages everything else.
-   Knowledge is the canonical output of Atlas.
-   Atlas Workstation consumes knowledge rather than source data.

## Closing Principle

> When there is doubt, return to the Ground Truth.

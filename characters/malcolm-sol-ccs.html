5 · Actions

Eldritch Invocations

Not spells. Always available unless the text says otherwise. They may change cantrips or grant free casts.

Agonizing Blast
On Eldritch Blast

Add CHA (+5) to EB damage rolls.

Repelling Blast
On Eldritch Blast hit

Push the target 10 feet away.

Eldritch Mind
Always

Advantage on Constitution saves to maintain concentration.

Armor of Shadows
At will

Cast Mage Armor on yourself without a slot.

Devil’s Sight
Always

See normally in darkness, magical and nonmagical, to 120 feet.

Fiendish Vigor
At will

Cast False Life on yourself at will as a 1st-level spell.

One with Shadows
Action · dim light / darkness

Become invisible until you move, act, or react.

Whispers of the Grave
At will · Speak with Dead

Cast Speak with Dead without a slot. Ref: 1 action · 10 ft · 10 min · up to 5 questions to a corpse with a mouth (not undead). Answers brief/cryptic; knows only life knowledge; no duty to tell truth if hostile. Same corpse can’t be targeted again for 10 days.

Visions of Distant Realms
At will · Arcane Eye

Cast Arcane Eye without a slot. Ref: 1 action · concentration up to 1 hr · invisible floating eye within 30 ft. Darkvision 30 ft · bonus action move 30 ft · passes 1-inch openings · can’t pass solid barriers or leave the plane.

Features

Class / feat powers — not invocations, not pact-slot spells.

Awakened Mind
GOO · always

Telepathy 30 ft with any creature you can see (they need one language).

Entropic Ward
Reaction · 1 / short or long rest

Impose disadvantage on an attack against you; if it misses, advantage on your next attack vs that creature before end of your next turn.

Thought Shield
GOO 10 · always

Thoughts can’t be read unless you allow it. Resistance to psychic; attacker takes the psychic damage you take.

Create Thrall
GOO 14 · action

Charm a humanoid; telepathic bond while on the same plane (see FG full text).

Alert
Feat

+5 initiative (already in Core Stats). Can’t be surprised while conscious; hidden attackers don’t gain advantage from being unseen (2014 Alert).

Mystic Arcanum

Each arcanum: cast once without a pact slot. You regain expended arcanum uses when you finish a long rest. Track each below (or use Ready/Used as a block reminder).

Arcane Gate
6th · 1 / long rest

Linked portals within range (see spell).

Etherealness
7th · 1 / long rest · Mystic Arcanum (not a pact slot)

Ref: 1 action · self · up to 8 hours · Border Ethereal. See/hear Material within 60 ft; only interact with ethereal creatures. Move any direction (extra movement cost); pass through Material objects as difficult terrain — ending a turn inside an object is bad (force damage + shove out). Ends: return to the plane you left.

Befuddlement
8th · 1 / long rest

Mind-breaking curse (see spell; formerly Feeblemind-class effect).

Page under construction. This legend will grow as Atlas Discovery reports land on the site. For now, the dots mean how sure we are about a fact.

"Mark what we know. Leave room for what the table will teach us."

🟢 Verified Grounded in Fantasy Grounds data or other confirmed sources.
🟡 High-confidence inference Derived carefully from verified facts — still open to correction.
🟠 Working note Useful at the table; needs a stronger source when we can get one.
🔴 Unverified / open Question, conflict, or gap. Do not treat as settled truth.

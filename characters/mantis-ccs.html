5 · Actions

Sorcery points and storm tricks — same spirit as FG’s feature list under spells.

Font of Magic
Sorcery points: / 5

Spend points for metamagic or to create spell slots (bonus action). Convert a spell slot into points (bonus action). You regain all expended sorcery points when you finish a long rest.

Empowered Spell
When you roll damage · 1 point

Reroll up to CHA mod (4) damage dice; must use the new rolls.

Twinned Spell
Cast single-target spell · points = level

Target a second creature in range with the same spell (1 point if cantrip). Spell must not be able to target more than one creature at its current level.

Tempestuous Magic
Bonus action · after casting 1st+

Fly up to 10 ft without provoking opportunity attacks (table note: BG3-style 30 ft if using that house rule).

Lucky (Halfling)
When you roll a 1 on a d20

Reroll the die; must use the new roll.

Brave · Naturally Stealthy
Halfling · Lightfoot

Brave: advantage on saves vs frightened. Naturally Stealthy: can attempt to hide when obscured only by a creature at least one size larger.

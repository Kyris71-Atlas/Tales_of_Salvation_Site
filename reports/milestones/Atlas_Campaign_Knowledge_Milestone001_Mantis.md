### Atlas Knowledge

Mantis is the reference implementation for Atlas Character Objects and
the first character promoted into the Atlas Knowledge layer.

Atlas currently knows:

-   Mantis is a player character in the Tales of Salvation campaign.
-   Mantis is the benchmark against which future Character Objects will
    be validated.

-   The "Twenty-Six Breaths" moment linked Mantis's Sleep spell to
    twenty-six sleeping miners.
-   Mystra became aware of the connection during that event.
-   Mantis repeatedly acted as a catalyst for campaign discoveries.

Memories from the River

In the Tales of Salvation multiverse, the Raven Queen became aware of many groups descending into Avernus across many versions of the story. Each journey left memories behind. Ravenborne was sent not merely to survive Avernus, but to reach the River Styx and recover fragments of memory from its depths.

At the Stygian Dock, Bazelsteen's bathysphere descended beneath the river to dredge silted souls. For Ravenborne, the descent became something deeper: he emerged carrying long-lost memories from the bottom of the Styx.

Discovered Memory
Atlas identified Memories from the River as significant campaign evidence before its full meaning was recalled. The discovery helped recover a piece of campaign history that had slipped from immediate memory.
